About Fresh Slice Pizza

Fresh Slice Pizza is a local food business dedicated to serving freshly prepared pizzas made with quality ingredients. We focus on delivering great taste, quick service, and a convenient ordering experience for our customers.

Who We Are

We are a neighborhood pizza brand offering a variety of pizzas for delivery and takeaway. Our goal is to provide affordable, delicious food while maintaining high standards of hygiene and customer service.

What We Offer

  • Freshly baked pizzas
  • Takeaway and home delivery
  • Online and WhatsApp ordering
  • Customer support for orders and inquiries

How We Operate

Customers can place orders through our website or via WhatsApp. We use customer contact details only to process orders, provide delivery updates, and offer customer support.

Our Commitment

We are committed to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Fresh Slice Pizza follows responsible data handling practices and complies with applicable business and communication policies.
