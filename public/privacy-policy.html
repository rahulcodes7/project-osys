Fresh Slice Pizza ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or place an order with us.

We may collect the following personal information:

Your information is used strictly for business purposes such as:
